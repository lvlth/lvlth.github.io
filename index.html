About Me

Hi, I'm Lavante. In May of 2021 I graduated from the University of North Carolina at Greensboro with a Bachelor of Science in computer science. My hometown is Fayetteville, North Carolina. It was there that I was first introduced to programming in High School where I learned the fundamentals of Visual Basic through building projects for class. I was also able to learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript prior to attending college. During my time at UNCG I was able to learn and build projects using a variety of programming languages including Java, SQL, and C. In my free time I like gaming, watching anime, spending time in nature, and making music. I love learning new things--I study daily--and I've found that within the field of technology there is more than enough variety to keep me entertained with new material as it’s always evolving. Although most of my experience is with web development and software engineering, one of my many career goals is to become a cyber security professional. I hope to one day obtain both an Offensive Security Certified Professional and Certified Ethical Hacker certifications, so in the meantime, I am strengthening my understanding of cyber security by utilizing online resources in order to train to participate in capture the flag events and to develop more secure websites/apps and programs. I am versatile, resilient, and most importantly, a team player. I'm not afraid of challenges, and whether I have the expertise or not, I am always willing to lend a helping hand and see things through.
